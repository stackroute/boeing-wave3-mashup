We provide timely, specialised veterinary care to injured urban wild animals, rehabilitate them, and then release them into their natural or adoptive habitats. Create awareness, amongst citizens of all ages and backgrounds, about urban wildlife, and to instil a deep regard for their cohabitation needs and when to actively engage in their welfare. Community outreach and education about our local environment and how we can enrich and protect it. Develop a practise that employs better wildlife care techniques, engaging specialists and veterinarians from across the world.
